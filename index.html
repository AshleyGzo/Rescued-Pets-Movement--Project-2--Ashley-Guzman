How Rescuced Pets Movement Works

Each year, tens of thousands of animals enter Houston area shelters. With most all animal shelters and other animal welfare organizations operating at capacity, RPM has been critical in dramatically reducing the number of animals euthanized each year at Houston’s Animal Shelters and other area shelters by transporting thousands of homeless animals to other areas of the US where there is a demand for them.

Nearly 73,000 Animals Saved Since Our Founding In 2013

More Than 1.5 Million Miles Driven

Average Of 266 Pets Transported Per Week

Our model is simple – The Greater Houston Metropolitan area has a very high number of homeless pets needing homes. Sadly, there just are not enough homes here for all of the homeless animals in need. We rescue Houston’s homeless animals from the shelters here and send them to other parts of the US. We drove over 1 million miles in 2020 alone, to bring homeless pets to their forever homes in places like Colorado and Virginia, and with your donation, we can continue our amazing, lifesaving work!
